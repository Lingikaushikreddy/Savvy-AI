# Savvy AI Press Kit

## Company Overview
**Savvy AI** is a productivity tools company focused on "Augmented Intelligence." We believe AI should be an invisible layer that enhances human capability, not a separate tool you have to manage.

**Founded**: 2024
**Headquarters**: Remote
**Website**: https://savvy-ai.com

## Product Description
Savvy AI is a native desktop application for macOS, Windows, and Linux. It utilizes multimodal AI (Vision + Audio) to provide context-aware assistance. Key use cases include technical interview assistance, meeting transcription, and automated note-taking.
